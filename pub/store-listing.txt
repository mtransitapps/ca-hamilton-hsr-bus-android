Full description:
This application adds Hamilton HSR buses information to MonTransit BETA.

This app contains the buses schedule (available offline).

BETA: Thanks for your patience while the last features are being added and the last bugs are being fixed.

HSR serves Hamilton in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit BETA app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit BETA app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by City of Hamilton and Hamilton Street Railway.
http://www.hamilton.ca/ProjectsInitiatives/OpenData/

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-hamilton-hsr-bus-android

This app is not related with City of Hamilton and Hamilton Street Railway.

Permissions:
- Other: required for real-time information
