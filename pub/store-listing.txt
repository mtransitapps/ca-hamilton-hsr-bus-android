Full description:
This application adds Hamilton HSR buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule (available offline).

HSR buses serve Hamilton in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by City of Hamilton and Hamilton Street Railway.
http://www.hamilton.ca/ProjectsInitiatives/OpenData/

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-hamilton-hsr-bus-android

This app is not related with City of Hamilton and Hamilton Street Railway.

- Other: required for real-time information
